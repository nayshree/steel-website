Hot Work Tool Steel

Metallurgically balanced to sustain structural tensile strength, hot toughness, and resistance to thermal fatigue when subjected to continuous cyclic heating and cooling.

Featured Grades

AISI H13 / DIN 1.2344

Highly versatile grade with premium toughness, maximizing service life in aluminum extrusion dies and high-pressure die-casting insert blocks.

AISI H11 / DIN 1.2343

Engineered with excellent structural resistance to severe thermal shock, deep thermal stress cracking, and macro-heat checking.

Tungsten-base hot-work grade built explicitly for extreme thermal loads in high-stress hot forging, hot shearing, and forming dies.
