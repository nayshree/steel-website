High Speed Steel

Highly alloyed chromium, vanadium, tungsten, and cobalt compositions engineered to retain extreme red hardness even at operating temperatures scaling past 600°C.

Featured Grades

AISI M2 / DIN 1.3343

The standard, highly balanced grade implemented globally for precision drill bits, industrial milling cutters, high-load taps, and metal broaches.

AISI M35 / DIN 1.3243

Formulated with 5% Cobalt to significantly boost thermal stability, protect edge keenness, and extend operational tool life during severe cutting runs.

AISI M42 / DIN 1.3247

An advanced 8% Cobalt grade offering superior hardness margins; engineered for heavy-duty machining of aerospace superalloys and high-tensile steels.
