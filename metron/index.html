Welcome to Auremax Industries

Dinesh Hardware Mart was established in 1968, followed by KMSI in 1987. As a leading Tool Steel Supplier, KMSI specialises in steel trading & stock holding, dealing in all kinds of High Speed Steel, Cold Work Tool Steel, Hot Work Tool Steel, Plastic Mould Steel, Corrosion Resistant Steel, Nitriding Steel, 2% Beryllium Copper and EC Copper in forms of Flat Bars, Round Bars, Sheets, Heavy Blocks, Tool Blanks and Finished Tools. We provide tailored products to suit any customer's special cutting & machining requirements.
